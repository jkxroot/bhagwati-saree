Our Story

A Local Name Woven With Tradition & Trust

For over a decade, Bhagwati Saree Center & Dress Material has been Vejalpore's trusted destination for authentic ethnic wear. What began as a small family store has grown into a beloved showroom for the women of Navsari.

We pour care into every selection — from the richness of Rajasthani fabrics to everyday dress material — built on quality, variety and personal service that keeps families coming back.
